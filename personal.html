About me

Since I was a kid, I have been an avid reader of science fiction. For example, I enjoyed reading The Three Body problem and Ball Lightning, and especially admiring futuristic works with high technology. Having been exposed to offline science and technology exhibitions and popular scientific publications such as Scientific American, I want to grow into a man like Elon Reeve Musk or Peter Bowman Scott-Morgan, who could change the world with more advanced technology.
